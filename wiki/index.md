# Wiki

The wiki is the brain's long-term memory. Plain markdown. Densely cross-linked. Enriched over time.

## Folders

- [[concepts/]] — cross-cutting ideas, mental models, methods
- [[people/]] — one page per person you interact with
- [[products/]] — your own products / projects
- [[partners/]] — organizations you collaborate with
- [[competitors/]] — organizations you compete with
- [[org/]] — companies, teams, org structure
- [[specs/]] — engineering / product specs (optional)
- [[design/]] — design system, diagrams, wireframes (optional)

Start with what you need. Empty folders are fine.

## Conventions

See [`rules/wiki-conventions.md`](../rules/wiki-conventions.md) for page format, cross-linking, and enrichment rules.
